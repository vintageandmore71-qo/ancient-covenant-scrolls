User Manual

Attain

A dyslexia-friendly universal reader and study companion for any book on your iPad.

1. What is Attain?

Attain reads any book or document you upload — PDF, EPUB, plain text, Word — and turns it into a clean, dyslexia-optimized reading experience with built-in study activities: flashcards, quizzes, fill-in-the-blank, and more, all generated automatically from the chapters.

Built for dyslexia: Atkinson Hyperlegible & OpenDyslexic fonts, sentence-by-sentence highlighting, voice reader, adjustable line spacing and color, syllable splitting on demand.

2. Install Attain on your iPad

  1. Open Safari → https://dssorit.github.io/ancient-covenant-scrolls/attain/
  2. Tap the Share icon → Add to Home Screen
  3. Tap Add
  4. Tap the Attain icon to launch — it opens full-screen, no browser bar
Storage: when iOS asks, allow persistent storage. Otherwise Safari may evict your books on memory pressure.

3. Upload your first book

  1. From the Home screen tap Upload book
  2. Choose a file from Files / iCloud Drive / AirDrop / email — supported types: .pdf, .epub, .txt, .docx, .md
  3. Wait for parsing — long PDFs may take 10-30s
  4. Attain detects chapters automatically and builds a table of contents
  5. Tap any chapter to start reading
Privacy: books stay on your device — Attain does not upload them anywhere.

4. The Reader

Tap a chapter to enter the Reader screen. Top toolbar:

⚙️ SettingsFont, spacing, color tint, reading-aid options

4.1 Dyslexia-friendly aids

  • Atkinson Hyperlegible font (default) — designed by the Braille Institute for low-vision and dyslexia
  • OpenDyslexic font — toggle in Settings if you prefer weighted letterforms
  • Line spacing slider — open up the page if dense text is hard to track
  • Cream / sepia tints — reduce harsh-white glare
  • Sentence reader strip — highlights only the current sentence; everything else dims
  • Syllable split — long-press a word to break it into syllables on a tooltip

4.2 Voice reader

  1. Tap 🔊 Listen — playback starts at the current paragraph
  2. Tap any sentence to jump playback to that spot
  3. From Settings → Audio: choose voice, speed (0.5×–2×), pitch
  4. Voice continues across chapters — auto-advances at chapter end

4.3 Notes & highlights

Long-press any text to highlight or annotate. Notes are stored per-chapter and you can browse them all from the Notes panel.

5. The Study panel

From any chapter, tap 📑 Activities to open the Study panel. Attain automatically generates exercises from the chapter you just read.

The 8 study modes (game-style)

1. FlashcardsFront-and-back cards from key terms in the chapter. Tap to flip; swipe to mark known/unknown.
2. Fill in the BlankSentence with a missing word; tap the right answer from 4 options.
3. Multiple ChoiceComprehension quiz from the chapter. 4 options, 1 right.
4. True/False with WhyStatement → answer T/F → tap the reason.
5. Mind MapAuto-generated concept map for the chapter. Tap nodes to expand.
6. Concept WebVisual web of related ideas. Drag nodes to rearrange.
7. Chapter TimelineOrder key events in the order they happen.
8. Cause and Effect MatchDrag-match: a cause to its effect.

5.1 Score and progress

Every activity records your score. The Library tile for that book shows total time read and average study score so you can track progress over time.

5.2 Customise an exercise

From the Activities top bar, tap ⚙️ on any mode to change difficulty, number of items, or to skip multiple-choice distractors.

6. Your Library

Every book you upload appears in your Library:

  • Tap a book → resume from the chapter you stopped at
  • Long-press → context menu: Notes, Share, Export, Delete
  • Reading time and average score shown per book
  • Search across all your books from the Library top bar

8. Sharing & export

  1. Long-press a book in the Library → Share
  2. Choose EPUB, PDF, or Standalone HTML
  3. iOS share sheet opens — AirDrop, Mail, Files, Messages, etc.
Standalone HTML: single-file, fully offline copy of the book + all your notes. Open it in any browser anywhere.

9. Tips for everyday use

  • Read in chunks: after every 1-2 chapters, run a quick Activities round — research shows recall doubles with active recall
  • Cream theme if reading at night and dark mode strains your eyes
  • Pin difficult chapters long-press → Pin to Home — they appear first in your Library
  • Add to Home Screen for genuine full-screen distraction-free reading
  • Family / classroom: AirDrop a Standalone HTML to share a chapter

10. Troubleshooting

ProblemFix
PDF parsing failsSome scanned PDFs are images, not text. Try a different PDF or run OCR first.
Voice reader silentSettings → Audio → confirm a voice is selected, and iPad volume is up; some iOS voices need a one-time download from iOS Settings → Accessibility → Spoken Content → Voices.
Activities feel too easy/hardSettings → Activity difficulty, or tap ⚙️ on the activity to adjust per-run.
Lost my placeAttain auto-saves scroll position. Re-open the book — it resumes within the chapter.
Storage warningDelete unused books from the Library long-press menu. Standalone HTML exports first if you want to keep them.

Appendix A — About Attain Jr

Attain Jr is a child-focused sibling app at https://dssorit.github.io/ancient-covenant-scrolls/attain-jr/. Same engine, simpler vocabulary, larger touch targets, brighter palette, 8 game modes specifically tuned for ages 6-12: Flashcards, Fill in the Blank, Rhyme Chain, Syllable Tap, Who Said It, Audio Fill the Gap, Story Sequence, Dictation. COPPA-friendly — no analytics, no tracking, no network calls beyond initial load.

Appendix B — Build plan summary

Locked deliverable commitment

  • Attain ships as a standalone PWA zip
  • Will eventually deploy to its own private GitHub repo, run on Cloudflare Pages free tier, and wrap as a native iOS app via Capacitor for App Store distribution
  • Free stack only: no hosted LLMs, no analytics, no paid APIs

Free private hosting (when extracted)

  • Cloudflare Pages free tier — recommended default. Unlimited bandwidth, 500 builds/month, custom domain free, no credit card
  • Vercel hobby tier — fallback only
  • Netlify is explicitly out per project directive

Compatibility constraints

  • Vanilla ES5-compatible JavaScript (no arrow fns, template literals, destructuring) — for iPad Safari support
  • Service-worker cache namespace: attain-vNN (currently v23)
  • Independent manifest, icons, start_url, scope — never collide with Load
  • PDF.js loaded from cdnjs.cloudflare.com (vendor option for true zero-dependency)
  • Google Fonts (Atkinson Hyperlegible, OpenDyslexic) loaded externally — small first-load cost

Open backlog

  1. Cohesive icon set across Attain / Attain Jr / Study (matches Load family)
  2. Voice cloning for custom narration voices (Piper / XTTS / LibriTTS)
  3. Standalone packaging script: turn current attain/ folder into a clean repo zip
  4. App Store readiness: NSFW filter, privacy policy text, encrypted data vault
  5. Multi-language UI (Spanish, Hebrew, Greek, Aramaic for biblical scholars)
  6. Family library — shared book pool with per-user progress tracking
  7. Daily reading streaks and inspiration cards
  8. Accessibility: auto alt-text for any images embedded in books
