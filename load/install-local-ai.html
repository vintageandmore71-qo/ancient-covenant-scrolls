Install Load AI on iPad

A simple, step-by-step guide

Your progress

0 of 6 steps done

Before you start

  • iPad on Wi-Fi (not cell data)
  • iPad plugged in to charge
  • About 15 minutes of time
  • ~400 MB of free space
Step 1

🔄Close Load all the way

Swipe up from the bottom of the iPad.

You will see all your open apps.

Do this: flick the Load card up and off the screen.

Then tap the Load icon to open it fresh.

Step 2

⚙️Open Settings

Inside Load, find the gear icon.

Do this: tap the ⚙ gear.

Scroll down until you see big words that say "Load AI".

Step 3

📦Find the model card

Look for a box called "On-device model".

Inside that box there is a purple button.

Do this: tap the button that says 📥 Install local model (~400 MB).
Step 4

⏳Wait — it takes a while

You will see words under the button change:

• Loading…

• Downloading 5%

• Downloading 40%

• Downloading 100%

Do NOT close Load.

Do NOT close Settings.

This takes about 5 to 15 minutes. Keep the iPad awake.

Step 5

✅Done — you'll see a green check

When you see ✓ Installed, it's ready.

A small checkbox turns on by itself.

Tip: the button will say "Installed" and turn grey. That means you don't need to do it again.
Step 6

💬Ask it something

Tap the 💬 speech bubble in the top bar.

Type a question. Like:

• "Help me fix this code."

• "Write me a simple web page."

• "Explain what this file does."

The first answer takes about 10 to 25 seconds. That's normal — it's thinking on your iPad.

🎉 You did it!

Load AI is now on your iPad.

It works offline. Nothing leaves your device.

Next time you open Load, it will remember.

Next time you open Load:
You do NOT install again. You will see "Reloading cached model…" for a few seconds. Then it's ready.
