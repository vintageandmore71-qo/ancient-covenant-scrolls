BUILD MARKER: BUDGET-GUARD-v1-2026-05-07-v17g44
Reliability addendum #8. Future-safe guard. Paid providers (cloud-optional rows whose registry capability list does not include free) are off by default; even after you flip the toggle, calls are still blocked when adding the estimated cost would push this calendar month past your USD cap. Load never auto-calls a paid provider. Free-tier cloud providers (Pollinations, Hugging Face, Cloudflare, OpenRouter free models, etc.) are not affected by this guard.
