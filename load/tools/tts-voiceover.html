TTS Voiceover

How it works

Browser text-to-speech → saved audio file

Type or paste text, pick a browser voice + speed, tap Speak & record. The Web Speech synthesizer plays through your speakers while a parallel MediaRecorder captures it through your microphone. Save the result as WAV / WebM. Useful when you want narration without your own voice.

How recording works: Browser TTS doesn't expose its audio stream directly. To save it, this tool plays the speech aloud and captures the room audio through your microphone. Use a quiet room, set device volume reasonably high, and don't speak yourself during the take.
iPad Safari may ask for microphone permission first. The captured file is created locally; nothing is uploaded.
