How it works

Read at your own pace, one word at a time

Rapid Serial Visual Presentation flashes one word at a time at a centered focus point. Eliminates eye-tracking effort — many dyslexic readers find this much easier than line-scanning. Adjustable WPM, pauses on punctuation, jump back / forward.
