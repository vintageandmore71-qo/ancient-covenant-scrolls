Install LoadStudio on your iPad

No App Store needed. Add LoadStudio to your home screen and it opens fullscreen, with offline support, just like any other app.

  1. Make sure you're viewing this in Safari (not in another app's web view).
  2. Tap the Share button at the top of Safari.
  3. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen.
  4. Keep the name as "LoadStudio" (or rename it), then tap Add.
  5. Open it from your home screen anytime. Works with Airplane Mode on. No tracking.
Page is zoomed in. Pinch out to reset, or tap Fix.
