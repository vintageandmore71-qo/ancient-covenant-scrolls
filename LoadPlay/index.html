Install LoadPlay on your iPad

Tap an icon on your home screen — LoadPlay opens fullscreen. No App Store needed.

  1. In Safari, tap the Share icon (square with arrow up).
  2. Scroll down the share sheet.
  3. Tap Add to Home Screen.
  4. Keep the name LoadPlay (or change it), then tap Add.

After that: tap the LoadPlay icon to launch fullscreen — works with Airplane Mode on.
